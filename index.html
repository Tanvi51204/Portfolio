Image Forgery Detection

A deep learning system to detect digitally tampered images — copy-move and splicing forgeries that traditional methods struggle to catch. Uses knowledge distillation to balance accuracy with real-world deployability on resource-constrained devices.

  • ResNet18 + CBAM teacher model trained on CASIA 2.0 dataset
  • Knowledge distillation → lightweight MobileNetV3 student model
  • Evaluated on accuracy, F1-score, AUC-ROC & latency
